<p> As the capstone project for MAE 3780, Mechatronics, I worked with a small group to create an autonomous robot. The robot was designed to compete against other teams to collect the most blocks in one minute. We utilized an arduino uno and a TCS3200 color sensor for electrical components. On the mechanical side the robot used a simple two wheel drive design and had two cardboard "arms" extruding from the back to help collect blocks. Our design philosophy revolved around creating the simplest possible systems. This strategy proved extremely effective, allowing us to troubleshoot quickly and stay efficient in the design process. In the competiton, our robot won many matches due to the opposing teams "beating themselves." Other robots had complex sequences that would often lead them to drive out of the arena and lose blocks after already collecting them. In a field of about three dozen, our robot managed to make it to the playoffs and place top 8. 
</p>
